Privacy Policy for Student Intelligent System

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Student Intelligent System ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our educational management application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • User Credentials: Username, password (encrypted), and user type (Admin, Teacher, Student)
  • Profile Information: Name, email address, and contact details
  • Academic Data: Attendance records, subject information, grades, and class schedules

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information and operating system details
  • App usage data and error logs
  • Login timestamps and session information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and manage access controls
  • Track student attendance and academic performance
  • Enable teachers to manage subjects and classes
  • Allow administrators to oversee the educational system
  • Sync data across devices for authorized users
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Passwords are encrypted before storage

Cloud Storage (Supabase)

  • User credentials and academic data are synchronized with Supabase
  • All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • We implement foreign key constraints to maintain data integrity
  • Access to cloud data requires authentication

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your information for purposes other than educational management

We MAY share data:

  • With authorized teachers and administrators within your institution
  • When required by law or legal process

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the system
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of data synchronization (local-only mode)

Data Retention

  • Active user data is retained while your account is in use
  • Deleted accounts and associated data are permanently removed within 30 days
  • Attendance records may be retained for academic record-keeping purposes

Third-Party Services

This app uses:

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication services

Please review Supabase's privacy policy at: https://supabase.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for educational institutions. If used by students under 13, parental consent and institutional authorization are required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or email.
